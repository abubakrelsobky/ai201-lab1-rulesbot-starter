CS50 is free, you don't need to pay for the certificate.

And, is it beginner friendly? Sort of... i guess. It is meant for beginners, but some of the problem sets there are really hard for beginners. Not impossible though.

But it is also where I started, and it definitely helped improve a ton. I think that the difficulty is what makes this course fun and really good, as you can definitely see yourself improving as you move on the weeks.

I highly recommend it!

Do the course for improving your skills though, not for the certificate. The final project you'll do there is probably worth more than the certificate.
