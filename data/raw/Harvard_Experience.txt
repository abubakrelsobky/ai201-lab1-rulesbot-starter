The rankings really only affect graduate programs. For undergraduates, there probably aren’t big differences in the quality of education of top 50 schools. The differences are elsewhere. The quality of undergraduate instruction is probably similar between Stanford and Villanova. The differences are selectivity going in and recruiting going out. I chose Vianova,because it is ranked about 50. In addition, I did my undergraduate work there I’ve also either studied at or taught at four elite schools. Harvard’s undergraduate CS program is fine, snd the recruiting is as good as any school.
